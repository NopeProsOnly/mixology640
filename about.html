The Craftsman

Behind the Bar

Welcome to Mixology640. Over the past nine years, I’ve built my career on a foundation of precision and high-level service. It's a standard that demands sharp attention to detail, which naturally evolved into my current passion: custom craftsmanship and the art of mixology.

To me, making a great drink is about intentional design. It requires more than just following a recipe—it takes a deep understanding of flavor balance, technique, and high-quality ingredients. I apply that same disciplined mindset to everything I do behind the bar, ensuring that what ends up in your glass is nothing short of exceptional.

My goal is simple: to provide a distinctly memorable, premium experience for every client. Whether that means designing a bespoke build from the ground up or pouring for guests at a private event, I bring a commitment to quality that you can taste and feel in every single detail.
